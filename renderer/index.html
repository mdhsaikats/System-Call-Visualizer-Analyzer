Perfect Cold Coffee ☕❄️

Make café-style iced coffee at home using your espresso machine. Smooth, creamy, and refreshing.

☕ Classic Iced Latte

  • • 1–2 shots espresso
  • • Cold milk
  • • Ice cubes
  • • Sugar (optional)

Clean, smooth and refreshing coffee experience.

🍦 Creamy Cold Coffee

  • • Espresso shot
  • • Cold milk
  • • Sugar
  • • Ice cubes
  • • Optional ice cream

Thick, frothy café-style blended coffee.

How to Make It

1️⃣

Brew fresh espresso using your machine.

2️⃣

Fill glass with ice and cold milk.

3️⃣

Pour espresso slowly over milk.

4️⃣

Stir or blend for creamy texture.
